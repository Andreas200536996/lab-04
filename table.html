Characters

The Seven Deadly Sins

Name Description Photo
Meliodas The captain of the Seven Deadly Sins and The Dragon Sin of Wrath, he leads the Sins into many battles as a fearless leader while also managing to keep the humour light. He often brawls with Ban, another Sin, for fun.
Ban The Fox Sin of Greed of The Seven Deadly Sins, Ban is immortal after drinking from the immortality pool in the Fairy Kingdom, and he often spars with Meliodas for fun to try and prove who is the strongest Sin.
Merlin The Boar Sin of Gluttony of The Seven Deadly Sins, Merlin is a mage who has an endless amount of tricks up her sleeve to aid in both battle with the Sins and her own personal research.
Escanor The most powerful member of the Seven Deadly Sins and the Lion Sin of Pride, the skys the limit for what Escanor can do. Powered by the sun, Escanor is most powerful at noon at which point, he bcomes the strongest being alive.
King The King of the Fairies and The Grizzly Sin of Sloth of the Seven Deadly Sins, King's Spirit Spear Chastiefol has many trasformations that King utilizes in battle to always maintain the advantage.
Diane The Serpent Sin of Envy of the Seven Deadly Sins, Diane is a Giant who loves the members of her team and will do anything to protect them, no matter who or what she has to fight.
Gowther The Goat Sin of Lust of the Seven Deadly Sins, Gowther contains the powerful ability to enter and control the minds of his opponents. Gowther uses this power to nullify the movements of his opponents and prvent them from laying a finger on him.
