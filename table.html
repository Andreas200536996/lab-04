Characters

The Seven Deadly Sins

Name Description Photo
Meliodas The captain of the Seven Deadly Sins, he leads the Sins into many battles as a fearless leader while also managing to keep the humour light. He often brawls with Ban, another Sin, for fun.
Ban A member of The Seven Deadly Sins, Ban is immortal after drinking from the immortality pool in the Fairy Kingdom, and he often spars with Meliodas for fun to try and prove who is the strongest Sin.
Merlin A powerful member of The Seven Deadly Sins, Merlin is a mage who has an endless amount of tricks up her sleeve to aid in both battle with the Sins and her own personal research.
Escanor The most powerful member of the Seven Deadly Sins, the skys the limit for what Escanor can do. Powered by the sun, Escanor is most powerful at noon at which point, he bcomes the strongest being alive.
